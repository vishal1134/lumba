NAME: LUCY

Then lucy periya ponnu aagi kutty potuchu andha kutties elaam erandhu pochu then again kutty potuchu adhula neraya kutty erandhu pochu adhula survive aanadhu 2 kutties MEENA and RAMBA. NAME: RAMBA
